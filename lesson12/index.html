Purpose:

The Chamber of Commerce of Cúcuta is an autonomous, non-profit entity, made up of people who do business (Industrialists, Merchants, Farmers, Ranchers, etc.), whose primary objective is to be the depositary of public trust, corresponding to it to carry the Mercantile Registry, which is the most valuable source of information for the life of business in a way that is an effective and positive factor in the organization of commercial activity.

Goals:

  • Defending business interests
  • Education,Empower, Encourage and support the development of business environments
  • Guide, as an advisory body to the Municipal Government, in defining the municipality's holiday calendar and in matters of interest to the business community.
  • Work with government agencies, form partnerships and formulate actions to strengthen the class and the business of its members.
